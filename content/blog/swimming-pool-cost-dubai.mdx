## What Drives Swimming Pool Cost in Dubai

### 1. Pool Type

**Skimmer pools** are the most affordable. Water drains through side skimmers — simpler engineering, lower equipment cost.

**Overflow / infinity pools** cost significantly more. Water flows over a weir edge into a balance tank, then recirculates. The balance tank, additional pumps, and precision levelling all add cost. Infinity pools facing Dubai skyline or sea views are among the most requested luxury features for villas in Palm Jumeirah, Emirates Hills, and Jumeirah Golf Estates.

**Rooftop pools** carry structural engineering costs that ground-level pools do not. The building slab must support water load (1,000 kg/m²+). Waterproofing requirements are more stringent. Access for construction equipment is limited. Expect a 30–50% premium over an equivalent ground-level pool.

### 2. Size

Pool cost scales roughly with volume and surface area:

- **Small** (up to 30 m²): AED 50,000 – 150,000
- **Medium** (30–60 m²): AED 150,000 – 350,000
- **Large** (60–100 m²): AED 350,000 – 700,000
- **Oversized / commercial** (100 m²+): AED 700,000+

### 3. Finish and Tiling

The interior finish is one of the biggest variable costs:

| Finish | Cost Range per m² |
|--------|------------------|
| Standard white plaster | AED 80 – 120 |
| Coloured plaster (Pebblecrete) | AED 120 – 200 |
| Mosaic tiles (glass/ceramic) | AED 200 – 500 |
| Premium Italian mosaic | AED 500 – 1,200 |

For a 50 m² pool, switching from standard plaster to premium mosaic tiles alone adds AED 20,000 – 55,000.

### 4. Equipment Quality

Pool equipment significantly affects both upfront cost and long-term running costs:

- **Filtration system**: AED 8,000 – 45,000 (sand, cartridge, or DE filter)
- **Pump**: AED 3,000 – 20,000 (standard vs. variable-speed)
- **Heating system**: AED 15,000 – 60,000 (heat pump preferred in UAE climate)
- **Automation / smart control**: AED 10,000 – 35,000
- **LED lighting**: AED 5,000 – 25,000
- **Salt chlorination**: AED 6,000 – 18,000

Variable-speed pumps cost more upfront but reduce electricity bills by up to 70% — important in Dubai where pools run year-round.

### 5. Landscaping and Surrounds

Most pool projects include deck, coping, and landscaping:

- **Pool coping** (natural stone, porcelain): AED 150 – 600 per linear metre
- **Decking** (travertine, porcelain, timber composite): AED 120 – 450 per m²
- **Landscaping** (planting, irrigation, lighting): AED 30,000 – 300,000+

## Hidden Costs to Budget For

**Civil works and excavation**: Rocky ground in some Dubai areas increases excavation cost. Budget AED 10,000 – 40,000 separately.

**Structural engineering**: Required for rooftop pools and some villa developments. AED 15,000 – 50,000.

**Dubai Municipality approvals**: Permit fees vary by project type. Typically AED 3,000 – 15,000.

**Water fill**: Initial fill for a 50,000-litre pool costs AED 500 – 1,500 via tanker.

**Ongoing maintenance**: Routine pool servicing in Dubai costs AED 300 – 800 per month for a villa pool, covering chemicals, cleaning, and equipment checks.

## Cost by Dubai Location

Construction costs are broadly consistent across Dubai, but site access and soil conditions vary:

- **Palm Jumeirah / Jumeirah Islands**: Slightly higher due to community access restrictions and permit complexity
- **Arabian Ranches / Damac Hills**: Standard residential pricing
- **Downtown / Business Bay rooftop**: Add structural engineering premium
- **Al Barsha / Mirdif villas**: Standard residential pricing

## How to Get an Accurate Quote

Pool contractors in Dubai quote based on:

1. **Site visit** — assess soil, access, existing utilities
2. **Design drawings** — 2D layout and 3D render
3. **Specification sheet** — materials, equipment brands, finishes
4. **Bill of Quantities** — itemised breakdown

**Always compare quotes on the same specification.** A lower quote often uses cheaper equipment, thinner shell concrete, or inferior waterproofing — all of which lead to higher long-term costs.
