Most Dubai pools need significant renovation between 8 and 15 years of age. Tiles fade, equipment fails, waterproofing degrades, and design preferences change. Renovation is often cheaper than full replacement and can transform an ageing pool into a modern showpiece.

## Quick Cost Summary

| Renovation Type | Cost Range (AED) | Duration |
|----------------|------------------|----------|
| Equipment refresh only | 15,000 – 60,000 | 1 week |
| Resurface (replaster/retile) | 35,000 – 150,000 | 3–5 weeks |
| Full renovation (all systems + finish) | 80,000 – 350,000 | 6–10 weeks |
| Renovation + structural repair | 120,000 – 500,000+ | 8–14 weeks |
| Complete redesign + rebuild | 200,000 – 800,000+ | 10–16 weeks |

## Signs Your Pool Needs Renovation

### Cosmetic signs
- Tiles cracking, falling off, or showing efflorescence (white deposits)
- Plaster discoloured, stained, or rough to touch
- Coping stones cracking, lifting, or staining permanently
- Visible algae stains that don't clean off

### Functional signs
- Water level drops more than 1 inch per week (leak)
- Filter cycles taking longer than 24 hours to clear water
- Pump making unusual noise or running hot
- Heater failing to maintain temperature
- Lighting flickering or not working

### Structural signs
- Hairline cracks in pool shell visible underwater
- Movement at expansion joints
- Water seepage in pump room or basement
- Coping pulling away from shell

### Compliance signs
- Pool doesn't meet current Dubai Municipality safety requirements
- Drain covers non-compliant (post-2010 anti-entrapment standards)
- Electrical not bonded to current code

## Renovation Categories

### 1. Equipment Refresh (AED 15,000 – 60,000)

Replace pumps, filters, heaters, and lighting. Pool shell stays as-is.

**When to do it:**
- Pool less than 8 years old
- Tiles and finish still in good condition
- Equipment failing or end-of-life
- Want to upgrade to variable-speed pumps for energy savings

**Typical scope:**
- Variable-speed pump replacement
- Filter media replacement
- LED lighting upgrade
- Smart automation install
- Heat pump replacement (if needed)

### 2. Resurfacing (AED 35,000 – 150,000)

Strip old finish, fix any small cracks, apply new finish.

**When to do it:**
- Pool 8–15 years old
- Tiles or plaster degraded but shell solid
- Want to update aesthetic
- Equipment still serviceable

**Typical scope:**
- Drain pool, strip old plaster/tiles
- Repair minor cracks and surface defects
- Apply new finish (plaster, mosaic, glass)
- Reseal coping
- Refill, balance, and commission

### 3. Full Renovation (AED 80,000 – 350,000)

Renew all systems and finishes. Like a new pool inside the existing shell.

**When to do it:**
- Pool 12+ years old with multiple issues
- Buying a property with neglected pool
- Major equipment failures
- Want significant aesthetic and functional upgrade

**Typical scope:**
- Equipment replacement (pumps, filters, heater, lighting)
- New plumbing (skimmers, returns, drain)
- Full waterproofing reapplication
- Complete resurfacing (tiles or premium plaster)
- New coping if needed
- Smart automation system

### 4. Structural Renovation (AED 120,000 – 500,000+)

Required when shell has cracks, leaks, or settling.

**Common issues:**
- Hairline shell cracks → epoxy injection + reinforcement
- Major cracks → cut and structural repair
- Settling → underpinning or structural support
- Severe waterproofing failure → strip to shell, reapply

This is specialised work — requires structural engineer assessment and Dubai Municipality re-approvals for major work.

### 5. Redesign & Rebuild (AED 200,000 – 800,000+)

Complete transformation — change pool shape, depth, or layout.

**Examples:**
- Convert skimmer to overflow or infinity
- Add integrated jacuzzi
- Reshape pool (less common, expensive)
- Add new features (waterfall, swim-up bar, beach entry)

## Common Dubai-Specific Renovation Issues

**Salt damage on coping** — saltwater pools with natural limestone coping show degradation after 6–8 years. Replace with porcelain.

**Sun-faded tiles** — direct UV exposure fades darker mosaic colours over 8–10 years. Use UV-stable glass mosaics for long life.

**Equipment heat damage** — pump rooms running 45°C+ ambient in summer reduce equipment life. Add ventilation during renovation.

**Calcium scaling** — Dubai water hardness causes scale buildup. Renovation should include scale removal and water hardness management plan.

**Chlorine-degraded plaster** — old plaster pools show iron and copper staining from years of chemical imbalance. Strip and replace.

## Renovation Process Timeline

### Week 1: Assessment
- Drain pool partially for inspection
- Document all issues with photos
- Provide detailed scope and quote
- Approve final design

### Weeks 2–3: Demolition & repair
- Drain completely
- Strip old finish
- Repair structural issues
- Replace plumbing if needed

### Weeks 4–6: Restoration
- Apply waterproofing
- Install new finish (tiles or plaster)
- Replace equipment in pump room
- Install lighting and automation

### Week 7–8: Commissioning
- Refill pool
- Chemical balancing
- Equipment calibration
- Final cleaning and handover

## When to Renovate vs Replace

**Renovate if:**
- Pool shell structurally sound
- Existing layout works for you
- Less than 50% of pool cost would be spent on renovation
- No major design changes wanted

**Replace if:**
- Severe structural damage requiring extensive repair
- Want completely different shape, depth, or location
- Renovation cost exceeds 60% of new pool cost
- Multiple layered issues (structure + waterproofing + design)

## How to Choose a Renovation Contractor

**Verify:**
- Civil contractor license active with Dubai Municipality
- 10+ years experience with renovations specifically
- Insurance for renovation work (different from new build)
- Reference renovations you can visit in person

**Avoid contractors who:**
- Quote without site visit
- Skip structural assessment on older pools
- Don't itemise quote (suspicious round numbers)
- Suggest cheap shortcuts on waterproofing
